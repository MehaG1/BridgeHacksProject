How Recycling Benefits the Environment

Recycling is good because we reduce plastic consumption, save trees, and much more! Recycling is an important way for people and businesses to reduce the waste they make; it turns waste materials into useful things, and provides a way to take care of waste reducing pollution, conserving energy and creating jobs; therefor, benefits the environment and our quality of life.

Landfills and Littering

Currently about 2.01 billion metric tons of municipal solid waste is produced all over the world annually.An estimated 13.5% of the waste is recycled and around 5.5% is composted.This figure shows that we are in a tense situaion demanding more disposal and less production of wastes. Adding more on the list of wastes has turned earth inta a large landfill. The process of waste disposal not only destroys the quality of the product but also degrades the valuable resources.

How can you help?

  1. Reuse
    • Using a reusable bottle for beverages on the go.
    • Using reusable grocery bags instead of single use plastic bags
    • Purchasing wisely and minimizing the purchase of packaged food items.
    • Making a compost for biodegradable wastes.
    • Avoiding single use food and drink containers and utensils.
    • Buying second hand items and donating the used materials.
    • Buying in a bulk to reduce packaging.
  2. Reduce the use of wastes like plastic and other non biodegradables
  3. Recycle non biodegradables which helps reducing their demands.
  4. Repurpose wastes, like using a broken cup for planting.
